FAQ

What are Core Web Vitals and why do they matter?

Core Web Vitals are Google's metrics for loading (LCP), interactivity (INP) and visual stability (CLS). They affect both user experience and search ranking, so improving them makes a site feel faster and helps it rank better.

What is AEO and GEO, and do I need them?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content so search engines and assistants can extract direct answers, often via FAQ and structured data. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) makes your content easy for AI systems to understand and cite. As more people search through AI, both increasingly matter.

Can you improve SEO on an existing site?

Yes. I audit the current site, fix technical issues — crawlability, metadata, structured data, speed — and add the AEO/GEO signals that modern search relies on, then measure the impact so improvements are provable.
